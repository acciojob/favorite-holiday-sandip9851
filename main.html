Sandakphu


sandakphu is the highest peak in West Bengal, India, at an elevation of 11,930 ft (3,665 m). It's located on the Singalila Ridge in the Darjeeling district, on the border of West Bengal and Nepal. The summit of Sandakphu is home to a small village and is located at the edge of Singalila National Park.
